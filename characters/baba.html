During Baba's youth, she become estranged from her village during a competitive game of hide-and-seek.

Lost, cold, and afraid, a new family adopted her - a herd of sheep. There she was crowned the Baa Baa Bandit and met her wooly husband Sean.

One morning, Baba awoke without her beloved Sean by her side. Traumatized by her husband's sudden disappearance, Baba has ventured to Barovia in search for her one true love, Sean.
